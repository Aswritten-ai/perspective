PositioningThesis

21 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers how aswritten.ai positions itself, narrating an evolution rather than a settled label. The mainline reframes from a [\"better AI memory tool\"] (Narrative_PositioningShift) toward [infrastructure for organizational knowledge across stakeholders] (Positioning_1) (tracked Jan 2026), and ADR-002 fixes the market line as a [\"worldview layer for AI agents\"] (PositioningThesis_ADR002), explicitly *not* a semantic memory API. The differentiator is [one unified worldview, not three separate AI tools] (Narrative_positioning), with the org-vs-individual contrast doing the rhetorical work in [OrgMemory] (PositioningThesis_OrgMemory). Value lands as [day-1 productivity] (ValueProp_1), [zero knowledge loss] (ValueProp_2), [coding agents that understand strategy] (ValueProp_4), and [auto-generated content] (ValueProp_5). The buyer-facing translation runs from [the customer's chief architect's plain-language pitch] (Narrative_CustomerArchitectPitch) to a deliberate [worldview-as-product] (Narrative_WorldviewAsProduct) thesis. Multiple audiences and framings coexist here; the digest holds them open rather than crowning one.
