This hub tracks the evolution of aswritten.ai’s strategic identity, centering on the shift from a tool-based narrative to a [worldview-as-product] (Narrative_WorldviewAsProduct) thesis. The core positioning, codified in [ADR-002] (PositioningThesis_ADR002), defines the platform as a \"worldview layer for AI agents\" rather than a semantic memory API. This is reinforced by a [unified worldview] (Narrative_positioning) strategy that contrasts sharply with the [individual-focused memory tools] (PositioningThesis_OrgMemory) currently dominating the market. The trajectory emphasizes a [platform-first approach] (Narrative_ProductVision), aiming to [extend software development rigor] (Narrative_SIC_Mission_1) into broader organizational operations. While the [customer architect's pitch] (Narrative_CustomerArchitectPitch) frames the value as an expert-knowledge extraction engine, the [strategic framing table] (Insight_FramingTable) suggests that enterprise-level adoption may require a distinct vocabulary—moving from \"AI memory\" to \"institutional methodology.\" The current focus remains on [Seed/Series A CEOs] (Observation_PositioningThesis_SeedSeriesA), leveraging the advisor network to establish the platform's [strategic alignment] (RubricAssess_Strategy_ADR001).
