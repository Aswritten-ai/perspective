9 nodes in this area of the perspective

The hub gathers how secrets, identity, and compliance are handled. The spine is credential hygiene: the [target credential-vault design] (Narrative_CredentialVault) removes rewrite.clj injection so [credentials never ride the MCP body] (Claim_CredentialsNeverInMCPBody) and are fetched from an internal-only Datomic-backed API, with the [internal endpoint reachable only on the Docker network] (Obs_InternalCredentialEndpoint), not exposed through Caddy. Operationally this was exercised by the [stale-credential cleanup in dev n8n] (Claim_CredentialCleanup) on 26 Mar. Compliance is the commercial frame: [GDPR/sovereignty/ISO act as adoption gates] (Narrative_EnterpriseComplianceGate), [local non-internet AI satisfies HIPAA and VIP privacy] (Narrative_LocalAI), and [client data-ownership] (Narrative_DataOwnership) (licensed base ontology, work-for-hire deliverables, repo under client org) removes lock-in. Two strands remain unsettled: the [BYOK OpenRouter-only limit] (Narrative_BYOKOpenRouterLimit) (Mar 17) and the [Vouch.io agent-identity bet] (Narrative_VouchIdentityForAgents).
