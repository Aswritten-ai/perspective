9 nodes in this area of the perspective

The core move, established 2026-06-16, is that no consumer receives a pre-fetched blob: every agent and every human reads through the digest and navigates from there. [Navigation dropping context by an order of magnitude](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_NavigationDropsContextOrderOfMagnitude) was both the fix for flakiness and the architectural direction — one decision. [Extraction roaming without pre-scoped focus](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_ExtractionRoamsNoPreScope) follows directly: a memory can touch pricing, positioning, and a person at once, and pre-choosing a focus would blind the agent to two of them. [Steering guidance placed in the returned surface rather than each caller's prompt](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_GuidanceInSurfaceNotPrompt) proved the principle — cite and introspect needed zero prompt changes. By 2026-06-30, [a consultant who ran enterprise discovery engagements for fifteen years's independent HATEOAS/RFC 8288 framing](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_DiscoveryConsultantHATEOASParallel) converged on the same traverse-don't-big-query instinct from a linked-data layer. By 2026-07-01 the pattern had propagated into structure: [perspective responses ordered by volatility](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveResponseOrderedByVolatility) with a stable map leading and per-call focus appended last; [synchronous per-message integration](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_SynchronousPerMessageIntegration) to prevent drift; and [the digest browser as the human-facing product surface](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DigestBrowserProductSurface) — the human as consumer of the same collapse/expand path agents already use. The [content/commentary split](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ContentCommentarySplit) sits adjacent: read-model implemented, write practice not yet.
