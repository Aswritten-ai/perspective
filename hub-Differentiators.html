Differentiators

42 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub tracks the evolving definition of aswritten’s competitive moat, moving from feature-based differentiators to a focus on [decision archaeology] (Narrative_MoneyAnswerDecisionArchaeology) and [provenance-backed trust] (Narrative_ProvenanceCitationMoment). The core enterprise hook remains [cross-repo, cross-platform, org-owned memory] (Narrative_CrossRepoMemoryEnterpriseHook), which addresses the limitations of repo-scoped tools like Copilot. A significant shift in recent months emphasizes aswritten as a [citation engine] (Narrative_CitationEngineNotSynthesis) rather than a synthesis engine, prioritizing specific methodology over the platitudes typical of generic LLMs. This is supported by [module layering] (Narrative_ModuleLayeringComposability), which allows for compounding perspectives, and [introspect surfacing] (Narrative_ProductSurfacesOwnRoadmap), where the product identifies its own knowledge gaps. The narrative has recently integrated [design-token extraction] (Narrative_DesignTokenPerspectiveParallel) as a structural parallel to perspective extraction, while maintaining that [storing the decision process] (Narrative_StoreDecisionProcessNotArtifact) is the primary value-add over merely archiving artifacts. The trajectory suggests a move toward treating collective memory as foundational infrastructure, analogous to [GitHub] (Narrative_GitHubAnalogy).
