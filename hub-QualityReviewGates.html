QualityReviewGates

43 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub spans the review, evaluation, and verification disciplines that gate knowledge quality. The review protocol cluster — established by Mar 2026 — specifies [baseline-first comparison](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_BaselineFirst), [labeled shift intent](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_LabelShiftIntent), [sentence-scale comparison units](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_ComparisonScale), and [large-TX zone-scoping](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_LargeTXScale), with [warm- and cold-start entry points](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_WarmStartVsColdStart) feeding a shared core. Review convergence is framed as [sharpness on branch intent, not exhaustive completeness](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ConvergenceNotCompleteness), and [annotation is positioned as quality control](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_AnnotationAsQualityControl) — per-section during drafting, not end-of-document. On the eval side, the [pre-registered battery discipline](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PreRegisteredEvalDiscipline) (pre-dating Jul 2025) holds that smokes prove mechanism only; [the battery proves quality parity](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_SmokesVsBattery). That discipline was refined — not superseded — by the [iterate-batches methodology](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_IterateBatchesEvalMethodology) operationalized Jul 2 2026, with [downstream-use efficacy](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DownstreamUseEfficacyPrimaryInstrument) named as the primary instrument by Jul 3. The verification doctrine — [assert mechanism, never roster](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_AssertMechanismNotRoster); [verify content, not shape](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_VerifyContentNotShape) — became standing instructions after the overnight arc (Jul 2 2026), extended Jul 5 to [verify at the user-visible seam](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_VerifyAtUserSeam). The [dev→main cutover](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DevMainCutover_20260704) on Jul 4 2026 required explicit founder approval and closed with three post-cutover fix cycles. [Extraction evals](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_ExtractionEvalsAboveLine) are flagged as the most important product measurement.
