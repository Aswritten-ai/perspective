QualityReviewGates

16 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub spans the review, evaluation, and verification disciplines that gate knowledge quality across the aswritten system. The review protocol cluster — established by Mar 2026 — is built around five corrections: [baseline-first comparison before shifts](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_BaselineFirst), [labeled shift intent (improvement vs. counter)](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_LabelShiftIntent), [sentence-scale comparison units](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_ComparisonScale), [large-TX zone-scoping via TX summary → zone identification → targeted shifts](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_LargeTXScale), and [domain-first eye-chart scoping](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_EyeChartScoping). Two entry points — [warm-start (same session) and cold-start (PR-based, different session)](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_WarmStartVsColdStart) — feed a shared core, with Scarlet's Jul 2025 kickoff preference on record. Review convergence is framed as [sharpness on branch intent, not exhaustive completeness](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ConvergenceNotCompleteness), with [lenses pushing both toward and away from the branch thesis](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_LensesConvergeOnBranchIntent) to confirm directional correctness. [Annotation is positioned as quality control](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_AnnotationAsQualityControl) — per-section during drafting (Mar 2026), not end-of-document, because [text length limits cause silent failures on full sections](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_AnnotatePerSectionNotFullDoc). The [pre-registered eval battery discipline](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PreRegisteredEvalDiscipline) (pre-dating Jul 2025) holds that the bar cannot drift to fit the outcome. The [review flow is framed as peer review for worldview shifts](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ReviewAsPeerReview), not fact-by-fact checking. Verification doctrine extended Jul 5 2026 to [verify at the seam the user touches, not one layer above it](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_VerifyAtUserSeam), codified after four 'verified' claims on the Jul 4 release day proved one hop short. [Every saved memory ends that review round and shifts the branch closer to the target](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_EveryMemorySaveEndsReviewRound), with next-round lenses reflecting the updated graph.
