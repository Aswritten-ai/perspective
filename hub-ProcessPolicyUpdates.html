This hub collects process and policy decisions across the pipeline, CI, branching, and protocol placement. The earliest dated entry (Jul 2025) established [prompting in tool return values](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ReviewReturnValuePattern) as a product-wide shift, complementing upfront tool descriptions. By Mar 2026, [compile gate placement](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_CompileGateInCoreProtocol) was settled: the gate belongs in Core Protocol, not /start, after Scarlet's explicit ruling that nothing aswritten should have been moved into /start. Apr 2026 produced two durable architectural lines: [research branch as temporal isolation](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ResearchBranchTemporalIsolation) (branch-at-root, boring-and-works, provisional flags) and the [perspective-vs-implementation boundary](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveVsImplementationLine) (why in memory, what in repo). Mar–Jun 2026 added [snapshot pruning pre-pass](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_SnapshotPruningPrePass) as pipeline-independent and [incremental digest regeneration](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_IncrementalDigestRegeneration) as the only viable rebuild strategy. Jul 2026 brought a cluster of CI and branching decisions: [tiered CI coverage](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_TieredCICoverage) after a [amount]incident, [direct-to-branch as first-class write mode](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DirectToBranchFirstClass) with [try-direct-fallback](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_TryDirectFallbackOnRejection) on protected-branch rejection, [main as promotion target](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_MainIsPromotionTargetNotDevSurface) with daily heartbeat on dev only, and [hotfix path now gated](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_HotfixPathGated) by the test suite. Jul 4–5 2026 added [post-cutover fix protocol](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PostCutoverFixProtocol): dev-first, isolated-but-thorough tests, small follow-up merges — replacing the implicit assumption that a full heartbeat wait is required.
