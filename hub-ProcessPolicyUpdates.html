This hub tracks the evolution of process and policy, centering on the [perspective-vs-implementation boundary](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveVsImplementationLine) established in April 2026. This principle dictates that while perspective captures intent, reasoning, and directional logic, the repository remains the domain for literal configuration and specific identifiers, a distinction reinforced by [Position_PerspectiveHoldsIntent](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_PerspectiveHoldsIntent). Parallel to this, the [research branch as temporal isolation](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ResearchBranchTemporalIsolation) strategy emerged as a pragmatic alternative to complex ontology, favoring [branch-at-root](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Obs_ScarletBranchAtRootQuote) as a \"boring-and-works\" mechanism. This approach, supported by [Position_ResearchBranchMethodologyDecision](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_ResearchBranchMethodologyDecision), defers structural experiments in favor of stability. Earlier foundational shifts include the [product-wide pattern of prompting in return values](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ReviewReturnValuePattern), which integrates reinforcement directly into tool feedback loops rather than relying solely on upfront descriptions. These policies collectively prioritize operational stability and clear separation of concerns over experimental architectural complexity.
