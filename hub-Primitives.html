Primitives

12 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers the write primitives and structural definitions underlying the aswritten knowledge graph. The keystone design, articulated on 20260701, is the [content/commentary split](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ContentCommentarySplit): per-message integration is the content layer; reconciliation is the authored commentary layer. That split is already reflected in the read model but had not yet been implemented in the write practice at the time of recording. Alongside it, [reconciliation as a set transaction](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ReconciliationAsSetTransaction) defines reconciliation structurally as a transaction about a set of prior transactions or nodes — the legibility differentiator that surfaces the referent set explicitly to the user. The [resolve tool](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ResolveToolInverseOfCite) is the inverse of cite: it finds the nodes commentary is about, runs once per pipeline run, and binds each reconciliation to its own subset. Source is append-only; all merge and supersession live in the reconciliation layer. A structurally distinct child, dated 20260704, introduces the [rich transcription format](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_RichTranscriptionFormat) with a per-span acoustic signal sidecar. [Shared agent memory](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_SharedAgentMemory) and [conversational AI interview extraction](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Observation_ApproachPattern_1) appear as related but thinner entries. Three claim children — covering refract, scope, and TX-as-prompt definitions — are named but carry no content yet.
