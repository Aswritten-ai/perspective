This hub gathers the analogies and metaphors the narrative reaches for to make AI-collective-memory legible, spanning several registers. A developer register anchors the new to the familiar: organizational narrative that [branches like code] (StyleObs_analogy_git), evoking version-control intuitions already held by the target audience. A physical/embodiment register evokes coherence: AI identity as a [resonant body with a shape] (StyleObs_Analogy_Resonant) and sailing [telltales as worldview-drift detectors] (StyleObs_4). An organizational/economic register reframes expectations: [Google runs search on ~10-15 people] (StyleObs_GoogleAnalogy) to calibrate ICP scale assumptions, and the [cross-functional sales↔eng cascade] (Observation_CrossFunctionalCascade) to dramatize bidirectional immediacy. A humanizing register frames the tool as a [coworker that frees devs] (StyleObs_ScarletPMBurden) from PM-communication overhead, with the self-aware in-joke of [duplicating the customer's chief architect's brain] (StyleObs_Analogy_DuplicateBrain) grounding knowledge-extraction value in enterprise humor. Across all registers: analogy used to anchor an abstract product concept to something already held.
