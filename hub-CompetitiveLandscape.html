This hub positions aswritten against everyone adjacent by a single axis: rivals do orchestration or retrieval of what's already documented; aswritten does upstream extraction. [RAG pipelines] (Obs_CompetitiveLandscape_RAG) retrieve existing docs with no architectural opinion; [Notion/Confluence graphs] (Obs_CompetitiveLandscape_Notion) go silent when strategic knowledge lives in a founder's head. The [enterprise agent-platform field] (Observation_CompetitiveLandscape_2) — OpenAI Frontier, Anthropic Cowork, Microsoft Agent 365, Salesforce Agentforce, Gemini Enterprise, Glean — is framed as solving orchestration, not knowledge extraction. [OpenAI Frontier] (Observation_CompetitiveLandscape_1) (launched Feb 5 2026, Intuit/State Farm/Uber/Oracle) is cast as plausibly [downstream of aswritten] (Obs_CompetitiveLandscape_Frontier). Category framings: aswritten as [superset of AI-twin clones] (Narrative_SupersetOfAITwins) and as [contribution-side vs conversation-side] (Obs_CompetitiveLandscape_MultiPlayer). On price, the [trimodal landscape] (Narrative_TrimodalCompLandscape) (Light SaaS / Heavy Enterprise / Custom Build, dated May 2026) sits aswritten 2-10x under Tier-B assembly. [DreamDB] (Observation_DreamDBConvergence) appears as a near-peer sharing RDF/propositions/provenance, diverging on general-purpose-platform vs extraction layer.
