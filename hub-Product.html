This hub gathers Scarlet's product lineage and the through-line connecting it: extracting narrative from data to steer outcomes. The arc runs from [Populous, the first venture-backed startup] (Narrative_Populous) — a medium-agnostic communication system that later pivoted to hourly-worker platform Clark — through [Capitol's automated insight extraction] (Narrative_Capitol), where Scarlet was first engineering hire. [The Outwork] (Narrative_TheOutwork) marks the first productization of narrative engineering, aimed at individuals. The decisive transition is [Vouch, the manual prototype] (Narrative_Vouch): a hand-built \"narrative source of truth\" seeding AI with sanctioned organizational steering vectors, then replicated across an org. [aswritten.ai automates that Vouch process] (Narrative_aswritten) into an ingestion pipeline, making a single data source context-agnostic. Two input-philosophy claims sit beneath: [richer input benefits extraction] (Claim_MoreMaterialNotAProblem) and [voice memo as validated format] (Narrative_VoiceMemoAsInput). The repeated pattern across all: create a vector that produces a larger outcome within a system.
