SolutionArchetypes

6 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers the solution archetypes and target use cases the product addresses, mostly via skos:broader. The unifying mechanism is a compiled organizational worldview that downstream consumers query. Concrete personas span [AI-assisted development] (UseCase_CodingAgents) (coding agents read product strategy and past decisions from a snapshot, ADRs saved as memories), [content generation] (UseCase_ContentGeneration) (speeches, blog posts, brand-voice consistency), and [executive assistance] (UseCase_ExecutiveAssistance) (strategy discussions as queryable memories, cross-team alignment). Above the use cases sits an abstracted [onboarding-elimination archetype] (SolutionArchetype_OnboardingElimination) framing day-1 productivity through shared worldview. an infrastructure advisor flags [DevOps] (Narrative_DevOpsUseCase) as a still-untapped persona — collective memory of deployment, rollbacks, maintenance mode. One child, the [story-generation flow] (Obs_StoryFlow), attaches as related rather than broader: it describes the synchronous render plumbing beneath these use cases, not a use case itself.
