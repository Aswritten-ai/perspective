The hub traces a multi-front shift in how work, staffing, and AI systems are organized. In Jul 2025, Scarlet and Tony framed two interlocking moves: [advisory staffing as a structural alternative to hiring](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_AdvisoryStaffingAlternativeToHiring) — monthly fees ([amount]–[amount]) replacing headcount — and [perspective-driven development inversion](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveDrivenDevelopmentInversion), where the AI draws on the expert's perspective rather than the expert directing the AI. Tony's [execution-to-strategy reframe](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ExecutionToStrategyShift) (Apr 2026) extended this: AI restored IC status, making curation and strategy the scarce resource. By Mar 2026, the sales motion had split into [a demo-call prospect direct sales and Tony influence sales](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DirectVsInfluenceSales), with [a demo-call prospect's two-step individual-asset-to-org path](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DemoProspectTwoStepSalesPath) and a [trigger-gated equity partnership](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DemoProspectSalesPartnership) under active pitch. [Calibration calls were reframed as billable staff-work](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_CalibrationAsStaffWork) (Jul 2025), resolving the free-conversation problem. On the technical side, Mar–Jul 2026 produced a cascade of architectural decisions: [Expert single-stream worldview](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ExpertSingleStreamWorldview) eliminating branching, [Core Protocol vs. Internal Operations separation](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_SeparationPrincipleProtocolVsOps), [session lifetime decoupled from verification latency](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_SessionDecouplingPattern), [Fable/Opus session-boundary routing](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_FableOpusSessionBoundaryRouting), and [protocol enrollment over command](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ProtocolEnrollmentOverCommand). Internal compensation was calibrated to company maturity via [the alignment principle](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_InternalCompensationAlignmentPrinciple) (May 2026), and the [team bottleneck reframe](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_TeamBottleneckReframe) dissolved the solo-founder framing as traction emerged.
