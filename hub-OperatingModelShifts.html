OperatingModelShifts

20 nodes in this area of the perspective

The operating model has evolved from early structural experiments into a unified strategy of curation and expert-driven development. Initial moves in Jul 2025 established [advisory staffing as a structural alternative to hiring](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_AdvisoryStaffingAlternativeToHiring) and [calibration calls as billable staff-work](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_CalibrationAsStaffWork), effectively formalizing the expert network. By mid-2026, this matured into a [perspective-driven development inversion](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveDrivenDevelopmentInversion), where the AI acts as an agent of the user's perspective rather than a tool for direct execution. This [execution-to-strategy shift](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ExecutionToStrategyShift) redefines the individual contributor's role as one of curation. Technical architecture has mirrored this, moving toward a [single-stream worldview](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ExpertSingleStreamWorldview) for the Expert tier to eliminate branching complexity, while the sales motion has bifurcated into [direct and influence-based tracks](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DirectVsInfluenceSales) with a [two-step asset-to-organization path](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DemoProspectTwoStepSalesPath). Recent refinements focus on [protocol enrollment over command](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ProtocolEnrollmentOverCommand) to ensure AI compliance through understanding rather than coercive prompts, while acknowledging the [cognitive cost of altitude switching](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_AltitudeSwitchingCognitiveCost) between building and vision-pitching.
