Narrative PreRegisteredEvalDiscipline

4 nodes in this area of the perspective

Framed in Jul 2025, [aswritten-as-first-user discipline](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_AswrittenFirstUserEvalRigor) established the foundational bar: a pre-registered battery run in full with numbers reported counts as validated; informal smoke-tests do not. The deliberate revert of the merge — keeping dev as a clean \"before\" baseline — was the first concrete act of that discipline. By Jul 2026, [the single-run timing condition](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_IterateBatchesResolvesSingleRunCondition) was resolved: the final full comparison runs once, against the converged pipeline, after queued fixes (retry-repair, quote-suffix elision) and the prompt-cache dividend land — running earlier would invalidate results at full re-run cost. Simultaneously, [ADDENDUM-A3](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_PreRegAddendumA3) operationalized the pre-registration: every case record now carries its pipeline SHA, making mixed-version masquerade structurally impossible. Days later, [anchor-5 replay validation](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_ReplayMethodologyValidated) closed the methodology loop — May-era transactions replayed identically to fresh production, confirming the measured 0%→100% citation-offset gap reflects pipeline improvement rather than environmental drift.
