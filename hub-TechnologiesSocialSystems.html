18 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub tracks the evolution of organizations as steerable, generative systems. The conceptual foundation rests on [flocking simulations] (Observation_FlockingInsight) and the 2019 thesis that [LLMs are continuation machines] (Narrative_LLMsContinuation) rather than thinkers, establishing [narrative as a steering vector] (Narrative_SteeringVector). Industrial precedents like [Harmony Labs] (Narrative_HarmonyLabs) and [Aspen] (Narrative_Aspen) inform the approach to language-as-code.

Recent development has shifted toward the [Agent Computer Interface] (Narrative_AgentComputerInterface), which frames the boundary between human and agent agency as the core design problem. This is supported by an [MCP-agnostic architecture] (Position_MCPAgnostic) now validated in production, alongside [Front App] (Narrative_FrontHITLSubstrate) for coordination, [Vouch.io] (Narrative_VouchIdentityForAgents) for security, and [perspective shares] (Narrative_PerspectiveShares_Distribution) for distribution. The [long-tail perspective farming] (Narrative_LongTailPerspectiveAutomation) model, which automates advisor interviews, represents a move toward scaling these systems. Emerging threads include a [neurosymbolic marriage] (Narrative_NeurosymbolicKGLLM) of knowledge graphs and LLMs, and a [Fireflies-based transcription pipeline] (Obs_FirefliesWebhookQuote) for data ingestion.
