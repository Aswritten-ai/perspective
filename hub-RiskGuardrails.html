This hub tracks the evolving risk profile and operational guardrails defining Aswritten's trajectory. The discourse centers on managing [misrepresentation risk] (Position_MisrepresentationRisk) through radical transparency and navigating the [SMS operational cost] (Position_SMSOperationalCost) as a structural enterprise burden. Recent shifts emphasize the [builder trap] (Narrative_BuilderTrap), where ambiguity in the consulting offer creates speculative engineering, and the recognition that [sales is the hardest part] (Narrative_SalesAsHardPart) due to the disconnect between technical value and non-technical buyers. Guardrails remain critical: [canon-too-soon drift] (Narrative_CanonTooSoonDrift) continues to warn against premature conviction leaking into external materials, while the [direct ingestion anti-pattern] (Narrative_DirectIngestionAntiPattern) has been significantly refined, moving from a blanket prohibition to a nuanced distinction between raw ingestion and AI-summarized bulk onboarding. The hub also maintains a list of [open design questions] (Obs_OpenQuestions) regarding schema and tool interaction, alongside a [fragility hedge] (StyleObs_FragilityHedge) style pattern used to bound acknowledged risks.
