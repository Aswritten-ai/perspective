The opportunity is framed through three distinct problem lenses, none crowned. The [strategy-execution disconnect] (Narrative_FragmentationProblem) casts it as fragmented coordination across isolated tools where developers lack strategic context. The [existential IP crisis] (Narrative_IPCrisis) reframes harder — a paradigm shift from developer convenience to a threat-level business problem, where every employee tells state secrets to AI and models lift IP into training data. The [AI context-quality problem] (Narrative_SIC_Opportunity_1) frames it as output quality: high-quality AI work needs extensive context that models currently chase through search. Two related value props attach as outcomes rather than problem framings: [day-1 productivity for new hires] (ValueProp_1) and [zero knowledge loss on departure] (ValueProp_2). The hub holds coordination, security, and context-quality framings side by side; the value props read across all three.
