FeedbackLoops

11 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers the mechanics of how feedback enters and reshapes the product and its knowledge. Two structural loops anchor the space: the [save-memory/PR round semantics] (Position_ReviewMemorySemantics) — save-memory ends a round, merge closes the PR, a corrected review always takes at least two rounds — and the [five-step annotate→document→save→re-annotate cycle] (Observation_FeedbackLoop) where annotation surfaces unsupported claims and re-annotation confirms the shift. [Convergence is framed as asymptotic] (Obs_ConvergenceAsymptotic) accumulation of review evidence, not a binary threshold. On process, the [real-time per-section loop] (Narrative_RealTimeFeedbackLoop) (Mar 19) is held superior to batch review — errors caught before they compound, insights surfaced in the moment. A dense run of Scarlet corrections (Mar 19) sharpens product framing: [expertise on-demand not ambient] (Obs_AmbientBehaviorCorrection), [perspective vs local RAG] (Obs_CustomGPTCorrection), [expanding experts not replacing] (Obs_ExpertReachCorrection), and [Tinkerer's existing solution as barrier] (Obs_TinkererBarrierCorrection). Later signals: [text-for-topic precedent] (Obs_ScarletTextForTopicQuote) (Apr 8) as rationale for the research branch methodology, and [feedback/help tool as churn prevention] (Obs_FeedbackHelpQuote) (Mar 26). [Open rhetorical questions] (StyleObs_OpenQuestions) surface unresolved design decisions as bounded scopes.
