This hub gathers the mechanics of how feedback enters and reshapes the product and its knowledge. Two loops sit at the center: the [review/memory cycle] (Claim_ReviewMemorySemantics) where save-memory ends a round and a corrected review always takes at least two, and the [five-step annotate→document→save→re-annotate loop] (Observation_FeedbackLoop). [Convergence is framed as asymptotic] (Obs_ConvergenceAsymptotic) accumulation of evidence, not a binary threshold. On process, the [real-time per-section loop] (Narrative_RealTimeFeedbackLoop) (Mar 19) is held as superior to batch review — errors caught before they compound.
