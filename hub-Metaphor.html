This hub gathers the document's figurative language across two registers. Empowerment/control metaphors dominate: narrative as [the deepest lever from CEO to dev and back] (Observation_DeepestLever), as [backdoor, steering wheel, and fault lines] (StyleObs_Metaphor_Steering), as [steering vector] (StyleObs_Metaphor_Vector), and as [competitive moat] (StyleObs_MoatMetaphor). A biological strand runs parallel — [organization as AI organism with worldview as DNA, artifacts as phenotype] (StyleObs_1) and the [living organizational worldview] (StyleObs_metaphor_living). Against these sit threat/failure metaphors naming what goes wrong: [context squishing] (Observation_ContextSquishing) for black-box summarization, the [cultivation trap] (Observation_CultivationTrap) for lock-in, and [the existential crisis of modern business] (Observation_ExistentialCrisis). Structural and engineering figures round it out: [Three Pillars] (StyleObs_Metaphor_Pillars) framing use cases and [organizational change as code] (StyleObs_OrgChangeAsCode). Several children here are stylistic observations — [triadic lists and 'at the cursor'] (StyleObs_10) and [colloquial 'hand waving' for vagueness] (StyleObs_HandWaving) — riding under the Metaphor label without being metaphors proper.
