Narrative DigestAsRecognitionAid

12 nodes in this area of the perspective

Established 20260616, this hub frames the digest as a recognition surface — not an account of meaning — whose job is to let an agent route or rule out without expanding to discover. [Absence as no recognized association](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_AbsenceIsNoRecognizedAssociation) draws the sharpest line: a legible-absence is never \"nothing here,\" only \"no recognized association,\" and targeted grounding may trust it while creative exploration must deliberately distrust it. [Expansion as steering](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_ExpansionIsSteering) completes the pair: the bad case is expanding to discover whether anything is there; the good case is expanding onto material already decided relevant. [The index never gating the store](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_IndexNeverGatesStore) holds the anti-reductionist spine — a claim is available to be read as many things, so the digest need only get close enough via an old association. [Multiplicity lives in variation](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_MultiplicityInVariation), not a theme label, and that block is the load-bearing anti-reductionist mechanism. [The eval must not reimport reductionism](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_EvalMustNotReimportReductionism) by scoring anticipation of every reading. By 20260701 the arc extended into structural implementation: [visibility as invariant](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_VisibilityInvariantAreaIndex) (every hub named in every response), [richness vs. visibility separation](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_RichnessVsVisibilitySeparation) as the principled line making tier weighting compatible with compression-only, [perspective response ordered by volatility](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveResponseOrderedByVolatility) with stable skeleton leading and per-call focus appended last, [reconciliation as set transaction](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ReconciliationAsSetTransaction) surfacing the referent set explicitly, and [guidance embedded in the surface rather than each caller's prompt](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_GuidanceInSurfaceNotPrompt) — proven by cite and introspect navigating correctly with zero prompt changes.
