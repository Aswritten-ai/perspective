Narrative DigestAsRecognitionAid

10 nodes in this area of the perspective

The architecture of the digest has transitioned from a conceptual [recognition surface](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_AbsenceIsNoRecognizedAssociation) to a concrete [product surface](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DigestBrowserProductSurface), formalizing the human's role as a consumer of the same collapse-expand navigation used by the system. Central to this is the [visibility invariant](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_VisibilityInvariantAreaIndex), which ensures the [index never gates the store](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_IndexNeverGatesStore) by naming every hub in every response. To balance this with steering, [richness and visibility are separated](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_RichnessVsVisibilitySeparation), allowing for tier weighting without demotion. The system now organizes [perspective responses by volatility](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveResponseOrderedByVolatility), maintaining a stable skeleton while appending focus. Furthermore, [reconciliation is now defined as a set transaction](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ReconciliationAsSetTransaction), surfacing the referent set to the user, while a pending [content-commentary split](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ContentCommentarySplit) seeks to distinguish primary integration from authored commentary, ensuring [multiplicity in variation](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_MultiplicityInVariation) remains the primary anti-reductionist mechanism.
