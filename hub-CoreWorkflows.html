CoreWorkflows

43 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers the operational patterns aswritten runs on. The flagship is the session-to-document arc: [Interview → Save → Draft → Annotate](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_InterviewSaveDraftAnnotatePattern), grounded by [save before drafting](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_SaveBeforeDrafting), disciplined by [one question at a time](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_OneQuestionAtATime), and generalized as a [replicable pattern](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_SessionPatternReplicable). The principle that [user corrections outweigh initial hypotheses](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_InterviewBeforeSynthesis) (dated Mar 19) anchors why the interview phase precedes synthesis. Review is treated as recursion, not a special flow — [review is just another remember](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ReviewLoopIsRemember), with Scarlet's canonical statement as witness. A dated March 26 simplification collapses standalone tools into on-demand local skills: [Stories](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_StoriesRemoved), [Notifications](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_NotificationsRemoved), [Refract/Registry](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_RefractRegistryRemoved), and [Memories/Source](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_MemoriesSourceToolRemoved) all removed, keeping only the [feedback/help tool above the line](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_FeedbackHelpAboveLine). The [six-step pipeline](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Obs_PipelineSteps) and [six-step review flow](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Obs_ReviewFlowSteps) specify the mechanics. Review-comparison corrections insist on [baseline-first](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_BaselineFirst), [labeled shifts](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_LabelShiftIntent), and [small chunks](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Position_ComparisonScale). Sales/consulting flows — [two-call structure](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Observation_TwoCallStructure), [influence-first](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_InfluenceSales), [two-week engagement](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Workflow_ConsultingEngagement) — sit alongside. A May 2026 [two-pass ARB pattern](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Observation_TwoPassPattern) and a voice-memo gap-filling practice (May 1) extend the workflow into ongoing iteration.
