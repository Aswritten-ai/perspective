CoreWorkflows

51 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers the operational patterns aswritten runs on. The flagship is the session-to-document arc: [Interview → Save → Draft → Annotate](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_InterviewSaveDraftAnnotatePattern), grounded by [save before drafting](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_SaveBeforeDrafting), disciplined by [one question at a time](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_OneQuestionAtATime), and generalized as a [replicable pattern](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_SessionPatternReplicable). Review is treated as recursion, not a special flow — [review is just another remember](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ReviewLoopIsRemember). A dated March 26 product simplification collapses standalone tools into on-demand local skills: [Stories](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_StoriesRemoved), [Notifications](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_NotificationsRemoved), [Refract/Registry](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_RefractRegistryRemoved), and [Memories/Source](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_MemoriesSourceToolRemoved) all removed, keeping only the [feedback/help tool above the line](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_FeedbackHelpAboveLine). Operational rules run through it: [compile gates all work from Core Protocol](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_CompileGateMustLiveInCoreProtocol), [push after every artifact](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_PushAfterEveryArtifact), and [a gitignored ledger plus quarterback](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_LedgerQuarterbackPattern) for multi-session coordination. A [nine-step dev-branch test plan](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DevTestingPlan) validates the full cycle, and review-comparison corrections insist on [baseline-first](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_BaselineFirst), [labeled shifts](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_LabelShiftIntent), and small chunks. Sales/consulting flows (two-call, influence-first, two-week engagement) sit alongside.
