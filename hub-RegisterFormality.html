This hub collects register and formality observations spanning the full range from zero-hedging declarative to self-critical tentative. The dominant pattern is mixed register: [session-memo code-switching between formal analytical prose and informal verbatim blockquotes](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_12) appears across [agent-simulation documents](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_AgentSim_BlockquoteRegister), [design-collaborator Tokens sessions](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_DesignCollaboratorTokensBlockquoteRegister), and [Fable Window planning memos](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_FableWindowBlockquoteStyle) — identified as Scarlet's session-memo idiolect in which blockquotes carry primary evidence and editorial prose frames significance. A second cluster is technical-authoritative: [infrastructure vocabulary applied to product decisions](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_PerspectiveOrdering_TechnicalRegister) and [engineering-expert-to-expert register with deliberate informal asides](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_RegisterTechnical). A third is epistemic hedging declared upfront: [conviction status bolded in headers](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_DiscoveryWedge_CaveatEmbedded), [notion-level hedging register](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_NotionLevelCadence), [provisional flags as both content and form](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_ProvisionalFlagVoice), and [unusually self-critical strategic-memo register](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_EnterpriseShape_TentativeRegister). Outlier registers include [unfiltered profanity for emphasis](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_DirectQuote_UXIsShit), [parenthetical italicized meta-commentary on source reliability](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_ParentheticalAsides), and [zero-hedging declarative assertion](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_8). The [blockquote-as-deliverable-speech-act](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#StyleObs_BlockquoteForScript) variant uses the same visual idiom for a different purpose: signaling ready-to-use language rather than evidence.
