Scarlet's Vision holds the [steering-vector thesis] (Narrative_SteeringVector) as its conceptual spine — language, narrative, and meaning act as steering vectors for generative systems, the way velocity steers particle systems — and builds outward from there. The product framing is the [worldview engine] (Obs_WorldviewEngine): SMS is merely one thing it can do. The fullest articulation (May–July 2026): [vetting is the beachhead] (Narrative_CollaborativeConfigurableAIVision), but the real product is collaboratively configurable AI individuals deployed in pursuit of goals, trustable through citation and visible reference, applicable to every AI task on every vendor. The category word is 'individual' — the antonym of monoculture. The product metaphor is [AI as ship, human at the tiller] (Obs_AIAsShip). Two infrastructure ambitions recur: aswritten as [the first thing you install] (Obs_FirstThingYouInstall) (like git/GitHub) and a [marketplace of reusable worldviews] (Obs_MarketplaceOfWorldviews) — not just skills, but entirety of process and methodology. The July 2026 fundraising reframe sharpens the moonshot: [how many voices are in superintelligence] (Narrative_HowManyVoicesInSuperintelligence), not whether AGI arrives. The wedge is enterprise and regulated industries; the ambition is venture-scale on calm-company economics.
