DataModelLifecycle

8 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers the architectural design of a data-model lifecycle built on Git. The spine is an append-only, [Git-centric transaction model] (Narrative_RepoAsSourceOfTruth) — content committed before extraction, async processing via Actions — realized as an [append-only transaction log in .aswritten directories] (Narrative_SIC_DataModelLifecycle_1) with hierarchical compilation across ancestor directories. Social structure maps onto the filesystem: [Discourse category→folder, thread→subfolder, post→transaction] (Obs_CategoryMapping). Several type decisions firm up the schema: [Memory decomposed into Message/Provenance/File] (Decision_MemoryDecomp) to kill duplicate provenance, [Snapshot+Ontology bundled into one State type] (Decision_StateAbstraction) for caching and synchronization, and a [batch of six open questions resolved] (Decision_ResolvedQuestions). Above the engineering sits a generative framing — [worldview as source of truth, everything else a compiled target] (Narrative_CompilationTargets) — and a philosophical sibling, [AI identity as resonant body] (Concept_AIIdentity).
