DataModelLifecycle

5 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub tracks the architectural evolution of a Git-based data-model lifecycle, where the repository serves as the primary source of truth for all content prior to extraction. The system relies on an [append-only transaction log](https://aswritten.ai/ns/narrative#Narrative_SIC_DataModelLifecycle_1) stored in .aswritten directories, enabling hierarchical compilation across ancestor nodes. This engineering framework is deeply coupled with a [Git-centric architecture](https://aswritten.ai/ns/narrative#Narrative_RepoAsSourceOfTruth) that leverages GitHub Actions for asynchronous processing. Social organization is explicitly mapped onto this structure, with [Discourse categories and threads mirrored as filesystem folders](https://aswritten.ai/ns/narrative#Obs_CategoryMapping). Beyond the mechanical implementation, the design is guided by a generative philosophy: [worldview acts as the source of truth](https://aswritten.ai/ns/narrative#Narrative_CompilationTargets), rendering all other outputs—code, apps, and content—as functional transformations. This is complemented by a conceptual framing of [AI identity as a resonant body](https://aswritten.ai/ns/narrative#Concept_AIIdentity), suggesting that these data structures are not merely storage, but embodiments of collective meaning.
