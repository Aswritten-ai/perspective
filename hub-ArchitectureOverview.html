ArchitectureOverview

41 nodes in this area of the perspective

The architecture has evolved from a directory-based compilation model toward a [transform-based perspective engine](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_TransformOverQuery), prioritizing [pure-function workflows](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PureFunctionArchitecture) where orchestrators manage side effects. Identity is anchored to [phone numbers](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Arch_IdentityViaPhone), with [group-specific repositories](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Arch_GroupAsRepo) serving as the unit of institutional memory. Recent refinements (Jul 2026) have introduced a [four-section response structure](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveResponseOrderedByVolatility) ordered by volatility, ensuring [visibility via a complete area index](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_VisibilityInvariantAreaIndex) while allocating [rich-tier slots based on steering weight](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_RichTierBySteeringWeight). The system now treats [navigation as the primary read path](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_NavigationReplacesEmbeddedSnapshot), moving away from embedded snapshots. While the [core ontology is domain-agnostic](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_OntologyDomainAgnostic), the [conviction stack's lack of a temporality axis](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ConvictionStackTemporalityGap) remains a critical structural gap, validated by production failures. The architecture also explores [named advisor perspectives](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_NamedPerspectiveArchitecture) as sub-agents and [in-memory graph storage](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_GraphInMemoryStorage) to decouple from GitHub dependencies.
