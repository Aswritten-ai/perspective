ArchitectureOverview

57 nodes in this area of the perspective

The architecture rests on a few settled primitives: [phone number as sole identity](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Arch_IdentityViaPhone) (no accounts, no OAuth), [one repo per named group](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Arch_GroupAsRepo) owned by a service account, and [GitHub as version-controlled institutional memory](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_GitHubAsInstitutionalMemory) — though a Jul 2026 refinement makes [GitHub optional by keeping the graph in memory](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_GraphInMemoryStorage) until the user downloads it. Workflow design follows a [pure-function discipline](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PureFunctionArchitecture): LLM workflows return structured data only; [orchestrators own all side effects at the edges](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Claim_PureFunctionSettled), with the [proxy as the single repo-IO boundary](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ProxyMediatedRepoIOBoundary) dispatching between GitHub and filesystem git. Perspective compilation moved from directory-based compiles through a query framing to a [transform model](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_TransformOverQuery) (Apr 2026), with [actor lenses](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_CompileTimeActorLens) identified as a missing compile-time feature. The response structure was redesigned Jul 2026 into [four sections ordered by volatility](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_PerspectiveResponseOrderedByVolatility), with [rich-tier slots allocated by steering weight](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_RichTierBySteeringWeight) and [visibility guaranteed for every hub via a complete area index](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_VisibilityInvariantAreaIndex). The [conviction stack's missing temporality axis](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_ConvictionStackTemporalityGap) — deferred since Apr 2026 — was validated by a production failure in May 2026. The [digest itself is framed as a recognition aid](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_DigestAsRecognitionAid), with [navigation replacing embedded snapshots](https://aswritten.ai/narrative#Narrative_NavigationReplacesEmbeddedSnapshot) as of Jun 2026.
