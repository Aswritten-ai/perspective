PunctuationTypography

21 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub catalogs the typographic and punctuation conventions defining the project's prose architecture. The [em-dash] (StyleObs_EmDashRhetoric) has historically served as the primary rhetorical engine, functioning as a [pivot for clarification] (StyleObs_EmDashDefinition), a [consequence amplifier] (StyleObs_DashAsAmplifier), and a [rhetorical pause] (StyleObs_EmDash_1). However, a recent [prohibition on em-dashes] (StyleObs_NoEmDashPolicy) has emerged, framing the mark as an \"AI tell\" to be replaced by standard punctuation.

Beyond the dash, the system relies on [bold typography] (StyleObs_BoldEmphasis) to provide [scannable structural beats] (StyleObs_BoldBeats), [anchor architectural principles] (StyleObs_BoldKeyPrinciple), and [signal substitutive shifts] (StyleObs_SMB_BoldEmphasis). Other devices include [arrow notation] (StyleObs_ArrowNotation) for process flows, [parenthetical glosses] (StyleObs_ParenthesisGloss) for term definition, [italicized contrastive stress] (StyleObs_ItalicsForEmphasis), and [fenced code-block diagrams] (StyleObs_CodeBlockArchDiagram) that treat architecture as a static, readable artifact. The collection reflects a transition from using typography as a stylistic signature to treating it as a controlled, scannable interface for decision-making.
