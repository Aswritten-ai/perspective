Narrative SeparationPrincipleProductVsOrg

10 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers a single dated demolition pass (Mar 2026) enacting one principle: collapse the product/org split by [eliminating the admin tier] (Claim_AdminTierEliminated) — everything formerly admin-only becomes either an org-repo Claude Code skill or 'read the files,' yielding one tool set for everyone. Whole systems are pulled from the product: [Stories] (Claim_StoriesRemoved), [Notifications] (Claim_NotificationsRemoved), the [Refract tool and registry] (Claim_RefractRegistryRemovedFromProduct), the [Memories/Source tool] (Claim_MemoriesSourceToolRemoved), and [Discoveries] (Claim_DiscoveriesRemoved). Their former functions re-land as local skills: [refract as a prompt-only skill] (Claim_RefractBecomesLocalSkill), [changelog/story generation on cron] (Claim_ChangelogStoryLocalSkillCron), [blog-post ideas invoked manually] (Claim_BlogPostIdeasLocalSkill), and the [graph health report] (Claim_GraphHealthLocalSkill) on demand or schedule. Synchronous remember now returns TX summaries directly, removing the main reason Stories and Notifications existed. Each removal names concrete n8n workflows to delete.
