This hub gathers how aswritten frames compliance — as both a revenue lever and an adoption gate. The mainline reads compliance as monetizable: a [regulatory-compliance EDO priced at [amount]/mo + [amount] setup] (Claim_EDO_RegulatoryCompliance), bumped from [amount]and named highest-value, anchoring the ~18-24 month self-sufficiency math.
