FocusSequencing

5 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers competing go-to-market sequencing theses — how to enter, what to prove first, and when to expand. One mainline argues bottom-up: [developer-first PLG] (FocusSequencing_ADR002) starts with solo developers and small teams on Claude Code/Cursor/Windsurf, then expands to executive and content use cases once value lands. A second route runs top-down: a [Strategy/CTO team pilot] (Observation_FocusSequencing_PilotExpand) of 5-10 people over 30 days, adding a second role mid-pilot to show network effects, then org-wide within 60. A third frames the beta as [content → people → process] (Narrative_ThreePhaseBeta) — prove with content, then add people, then show partners. Underwriting all of it, a [revenue tradeoff] (Narrative_RevenueTradeoff) commits to forgoing [amount]-80K to protect product-vision integrity. The [strategy rubric] (RubricAssess_Strategy_ADR001) names focus/sequencing as a scored alignment axis. No human reconciliation crowns a single entry path; the three sequences sit side by side, each with a different first wedge.
