This hub gathers observations of an authoritative register running through aswritten's documents. The mechanics recur: predominantly [active, agent-first voice] (StyleObs_ActiveVoice), [short declarative verdicts with zero hedging] (StyleObs_DirectEvaluativeVoice), and [direct imperatives stated as settled fact] (StyleObs_DirectImperativeTone).
