FrictionFailure

44 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub tracks the friction points in AI adoption, development, and sales. Development friction is dominated by [AI code back-pressure] (Narrative_AIBackPressureCodeReview), where generation speed outpaces human review, and the [AI action trap] (Narrative_AIActionTrap), an infinite loop of ungrounded execution. These technical hurdles are compounded by [technical adoption friction] (Narrative_TechnicalAdoptionFriction), where users fear workflow contamination, and [identity onboarding gaps] (Narrative_IdentityOnboardingGap) that leave agents ungrounded. Sales and adoption are hindered by the [activation gap] (Narrative_ActivationGap), where the product is misperceived as an investment rather than an immediate tool, and [two-sided legibility problems] (Narrative_TwoSidedLegibilityProblem) that prevent practitioners from communicating their methodology. Organizational memory failures, such as [context hemorrhage] (Narrative_ContextHemorrhage) and [day-to-day re-litigation] (Narrative_DayToDayRelitigation), highlight the structural [forgetting bottleneck] (Narrative_ForgettingBottleneckIsRole) inherent in decision-junction roles. While [value is proven by showing] (Narrative_ShowVsDemoProof), the path to scale remains blocked by [internal organizational conflict] (Narrative_StaffingAIAdoptionWall) and the need for [ambient adoption] (Position_AmbientAdoption) beyond developer tooling.
