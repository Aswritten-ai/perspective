VerbChoice

10 nodes in this area of the perspective

This hub gathers style observations on verb choice across aswritten.ai's pitch and messaging. The dominant pattern is a deliberate import of code/git vocabulary into organizational process: [recompiles as org-iteration] (StyleObs_3), the bare power verb [compile] (StyleObs_verb_compile), and the [notices/draft/commit/review/update sequence] (StyleObs_5) that runs git-parallel without demanding git knowledge. Alongside this runs a strong [active-voice, no-hedge register] (StyleObs_ActiveVoice) — \"We eliminate,\" \"We make\" — and clusters of [strong SVO action verbs] (StyleObs_ActiveVerbs) (unlocks, triggers, propagates) and [concrete economic verbs] (StyleObs_VerbChoice_ActionVerbs) (investing, leaking, cutting). The brand signature lands as a [phrasal verb, \"remember as written\"] (StyleObs_verb_1). Countervailing the technical register, a [colloquial validation phrase, \"kick the tires\"] (StyleObs_2) keeps the voice informal, and an [ICP quote] (StyleObs_ICPQuote) carries quantified, active phrasing.
